3d printing just got easier.


How it works


Autoprint is an accessory for your 3D printer which allows you to
print models you find online without downloading or slicing them.
It uses a small but powerful processor called a Raspberry Pi, which receives the link to the
model, then downloads and slices the model, and directly sends
commands to your 3D printer until the print is finished.


3 setup steps


1. Install our Chrome extension
2. Connect your APK (Autoprint Kit) to your Wifi
3. Connect APK to your printer via USB


Congrats

You can now print models you find online with (basically) one click!
