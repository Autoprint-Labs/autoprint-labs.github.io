3d print with Autoprint




How it works


With Autoprint you can print models you find online effortlessly.
If you have a 3d printer and want to speed up your slicing process,
this product is for you. It makes 3d printing online models as easy
as paper printing. How though? Our product sits right by your 3d
printer and automates the whole slicing process for you. It's cool.

Simple Setup


1. Install our Chrome extension
2. Connect your Autoprint Kit to your Wifi
3. Connect APK to your printer via USB
